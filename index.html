  • P3: Way of the Cards
    • This project was made by myself, Mann Gupta, Gavin McGinness, Bennett Sedlacek, and Saicharan Vemuri
    Technology used in the project: Unity, C#, Blender, FL Studio, Aseprite
    This is a action rogue-like deckbuilkding game where you play as a magician and gather your lost cards by fighting your way through a procedurally generated dungeon. Build your deck with cards of different elements to counter the different elemental enemies and make it to the boss at the end.
    I was responsible for or helped with:
    • The minimap
    • First iteration of enemies (later improved on by Gavin using NavMesh system)
    • Flame and barrel traps
    • Breakable items around rooms
    • Miscellaneous particle and sound effects
    • Various bug fixes, balancing, and juice improvements
